Juice and Jam – Big Arts Coast Breakfast

  • Thu 30 Aug
  • 7:30am - 10am
  • Pier 33 - 33/45 Parkyn Parade, Mooloolaba
  • $35 plus booking fee, includes breakfast
Book now

Juice and Jam – Big Arts Coast Breakfast

Presented by Horizon and Sunshine Coast Creative Alliance. Experience a Sunshine Coast icon revitalised and join us on the edge of Mooloolaba Marina.

Enjoy the expansive views of blue sea, seaside skies, swaying palms and bobbing boats at Pier 33.

This special breakfast  will bring people together from across the arts and creative sectors for rich discussion, inspiration and opportunities to explore new collaborations.

Breakfast entertainment by Horizon artists will include live music by Tim Loydell- The Bunker a performance by Chloe Lanham - FEMININUS, Live artwork creation by Nicole Voevoedin Cash and a slumbering Yeti-The Travelling Wunderkammer.

Hear from CEO of Screen Queensland and Arts Advisory Board Member, Tracey Vieira, about how the Sunshine Coast Arts Plan 2018-38 will shape and continue to grow and strengthen our creative sector.

Following this, award winning blogger, journalist, screenwriter, playwright and Sunshine Coast born and raised, Michelle Law, will be in conversation discussing how her sense of place has informed her own personal identity and more broadly how our landscape informs who we are, what we create and how we live.
