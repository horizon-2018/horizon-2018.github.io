Discover the Ancient Art of Cyanotype Printing

Join Swedish artist and experimental digital creative, Jonas Pettersson, and explore ancient print techniques and experimental mediums. Participants will learn Cyanotype; a photographic printing process that produces a cyan-blue print, as well as discuss ideas to expand current studio work across new-media.  

Participants will need to bring a minimum of 2 sheets of 300gsm watercolour art paper, paints and brushes. 

Note: Morning tea and lunch are included.
